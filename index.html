    BookAlive

    BookAlive is a mobile (android) application that is mainly targeted for young children to interact with books. Simply use the phone camera to point and click a page of the book, and interact with the image by

    • tap on character/object to learn about it
    • speaks out descriptions of objects, helping children read and pronounce
    • asks simple questions, like mark out the telephone booth in the page, helping children learn to recognize objects
